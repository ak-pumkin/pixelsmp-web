🌟 PixelSMP Features

🏹 Survival with a Twist

Classic survival experience with grief protection, PvE events, and quality-of-life upgrades.

💼 Jobs & Economy

  • Earn money with Jobs Reborn (Miner, Builder, Farmer, etc.)
  • Buy, sell & trade items at player shops
  • Dynamic marketplace with server balance integration

🎯 Quests & Weekly Challenges

  • Daily and Weekly quests with epic rewards
  • Track your progress via `/quests` command
  • Win items, boosts, and leaderboard fame

🧰 Custom Items

  • Craft or earn powerful tools and weapons
  • Special abilities and enchantments (e.g., AoE pickaxe, Fire sword)
  • Exclusive skins and glowing effects

🎮 Discord Integration

  • Chat between in-game and Discord in real-time
  • Verify your Minecraft account via Discord
  • Role sync and announcements
