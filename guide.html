📘 Getting Started on PixelSMP

🔗 Join the Server

Join our Discord server at discord.gg/ZJMAbkNFFG to get the IP and verify your account.

🪓 Starter Kit

  • Upon joining, use `/kit starter`
  • Includes basic tools, food, and the Pixel Guide book

💡 Tips for Beginners

  • Claim your land using `/claim` to avoid griefing
  • Use `/sethome` to save your base location
  • Explore `/warp shop` and `/warp quests` for quick access

🪙 Making Money

  • Pick a job with `/jobs browse` and earn as you play
  • Sell extra items at the marketplace or `/ah`
  • Complete quests for rare loot & money

📏 Rules Summary

  • No griefing, hacking, or exploiting bugs
  • Respect other players and staff
  • Keep chat clean and friendly
