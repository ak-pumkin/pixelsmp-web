📘 Getting Started on PixelSMP

🔗 Join the Server

Join our Discord server at discord.gg/ZJMAbkNFFG to get the IP and verify your account.

🪓 Starter Kit

  • Use `/kit starter`
  • Includes basic tools, food, and the Pixel Guide book

💡 Tips for Beginners

  • Use `/claim` to protect your land
  • Use `/sethome` to save your base
  • Explore `/warp shop`, `/warp quests`

🪙 Making Money

  • Pick a job with `/jobs browse`
  • Sell items at the market or auction house
  • Complete quests for loot & currency

📏 Rules Summary

  • No griefing, hacking, or exploiting
  • Be respectful to all players
  • Keep the chat clean
